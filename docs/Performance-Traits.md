# Performance Traits

## Rating Scale

Traits are scored using five fixed values, or marked **NOB** (Not Observed):

| Score | Label | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 1.0 | BELOW | Below standards |
| 2.0 | — | Does not yet meet all 3.0 standards |
| 3.0 | MEETS | Meets all 3.0 standards |
| 4.0 | — | Exceeds most 3.0 standards |
| 5.0 | EXCEEDS | Meets overall 5.0 criteria |
| NOB | Not Observed | Not observed during report period |

The form automatically calculates the Member Trait Average and excludes NOB scores from the calculation.

## Trait Definitions (Blocks 33–39)

### Block 33 — Professional Expertise
Professional knowledge, proficiency, and qualifications.

### Block 34 — Command or Organizational Climate / Equal Opportunity
Contributing to growth and development, human worth, and community.

### Block 35 — Military Bearing / Character
Appearance, conduct, physical fitness, and adherence to Navy Core Values.

### Block 36 — Teamwork
Contributions toward team building and team results.

### Block 37 — Mission Accomplishment and Initiative
Taking initiative, planning/prioritizing, and achieving mission.

### Block 38 — Leadership
Organizing, motivating, and developing others to accomplish goals.

### Block 39 — Tactical Performance *(warfare qualified officers only)*
Basic and tactical employment of weapons systems. Leave blank if not applicable.

### Rater Group Summary (Block 43)
The form auto-populates counts from all reports in the active database for five categories: Significant Problems, Progressing, Promotable, Must Promote, and Early Promote. These counts are included in PDF and ACCDB exports.
